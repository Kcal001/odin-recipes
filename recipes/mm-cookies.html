M&M Cookies

Description

Boiiii if you don't know bout these you mad sheltered! These bitches SLAPP!!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 8oz unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • M&M's candy

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Cream sugars and butter
  3. Mix in eggs and vanilla
  4. Mix dry ingredients and sift into wet
  5. Mix wet and dry till mostly uniform
  6. Mix in M&M's candy (However many you want... Feel it in your heart)
  7. Chill dough from 0-3hrs
  8. Line a 9x11" baking sheet with parchment paper
  9. Make and place roughly 1-1/2" dough balls roughly 1" apart on lined baking sheet
  10. Place sheet in oven and bake for 10-12 minutes
